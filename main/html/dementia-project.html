Role

UX Designer

Client

Dementia UK

Duration

24 hours

Project Overview

This application was designed for Dementia UK during a 24-hour hackathon, utilizing Lean UX methods to drive speed, adaptability, and the delivery of a comprehensive solution under a tight deadline. Despite the time constraints, we successfully created an effective application that met key user needs and provided practical support for caregivers and families of individuals with dementia.


Problem Statement

Families and caregivers of people with dementia often don't have enough information about the condition and aren’t sure how to help or support their loved ones.

Research

Competitive Analysis: Our research began with a competitor analysis, exploring platforms that provide information and support for users managing similar conditions. The analysis focused on comparing features offered by these platforms and identifying gaps in features that are currently unavailable.

Ideation

Based on the research findings, we initiated brainstorming sessions to generate a broad range of ideas. We utilized techniques like Crazy 8s to encourage rapid, creative thinking and explore diverse solutions quickly. Following this, we produced initial sketches to visually represent key concepts and page layouts, laying the foundation for further design development.

Our Design System.

Our design system ensures consistency and efficiency across the application. It includes a defined color palette, typography system, and intuitive icons, all aligned with the brand. The color palette creates visual clarity, the typography enhances readability, and the icons provide clear visual cues.

High fidelity wireframes

Based on the insights gathered from our research and ideation phase, we created high-fidelity wireframes. These wireframes brough our concepts to life with a clear, detailed representation of the final design.
